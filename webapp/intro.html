Tasks

Your task is to find the two vulnerabilities hidden in the webapplication and answer the following questions:

  • Search the prices and names of all future products in the shop.
    These products were not displayed in normal use.
  • Check if you could get other usernames and e-mails.
    Why is it not possible to get the passwords?
  • Post a review for a product of your choice in the name of someone else.
